Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025

The IPL is a professional T20 cricket league in India, featuring top Indian and international players.

All Teams

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians is the most successful franchise in IPL history with five titles, known for its balanced squad and strong leadership.

Chennai Super Kings

Chennai Super Kings, led by MS Dhoni, is one of the most consistent teams in IPL, famous for their calm strategy and loyal fan base.

Kolkata Knight Riders

KKR is known for its aggressive gameplay and two IPL titles. The team has a strong presence both on and off the field.

Royal Challengers Bangalore

RCB, though yet to win an IPL title, has one of the strongest fanbases and iconic players like Virat Kohli and AB de Villiers.

Rajasthan Royals

Rajasthan Royals won the first IPL season in 2008 and is known for giving young talent a platform to shine.

Sunrisers Hyderabad

Sunrisers Hyderabad is a team known for its strong bowling lineup and disciplined approach to the game.

Delhi Capitals

Delhi Capitals have improved in recent seasons with a young squad and smart leadership, aiming for their first IPL title.

Lucknow Super Giants

One of the newest teams, LSG made an impressive start and are building a solid reputation in the league.

Punjab Kings

Punjab Kings have shown moments of brilliance over the years and are known for their passionate fan base.

Gujarat Titans

Gujarat Titans, a new team introduced in 2022, surprised everyone by winning the title in their debut season.
